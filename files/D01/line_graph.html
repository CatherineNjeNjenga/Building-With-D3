February 3, 2025

Today, I recreated a line chart from OECD unemployment rate. The data was sourced from data.world.

Highlights

This is my first time building a line graph, and I love it when I can add an area to it. What I liked most about this session was the opportunity to use d3.scaleUtc, which should be preferred as it behaves more predictably: days are always twenty four hours are and the scale does not depend on the browsers's time zone.
